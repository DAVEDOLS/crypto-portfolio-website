Centralized vs Decentralized Exchanges

🏦 Centralized Exchanges (CEX)

  • ✅ Easier to use
  • ✅ Fiat on-ramps (bank/card)
  • ✅ Higher liquidity
  • ❌ You don't control your keys

🔗 Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)

  • ✅ Non-custodial (you control keys)
  • ✅ Better privacy
  • ❌ Requires crypto wallet
  • ❌ Higher gas fees on Ethereum

Pro Tip for Beginners

Start with a beginner-friendly platform like Coinbase. Once you're comfortable, you can explore Binance or Kraken for better fees and more features.
